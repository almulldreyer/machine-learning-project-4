Team by Team Model

Since this big model did not give us a very productive r-squared, we made a model for each individual team.

Some of the individual teams' models are much better than the combined, but other results are unintelligible. The R-squared testing score for the Dallas Mavericks is especially bad. We thought about taking certain data out, so that the combined model could get a better result, but 12 teams have negative r-squares which is more that one third of total teams.

Taking out a third of the data from the model seems to defeat the purpose.
